Tarot decks I admire but haven't gotten around to picking up

  • English Magic Tarot has this fantastic, flat imagery that looks simple on the surface but has a dozen stories.
  • Thelema Tarot has beautiful images that each seem to glow.
  • Lo Scarabeo trys to blend the two major lineages of Tarot, The Waite-Smith and The Crowley Thoth.
  • Visconti-Sforza Tarot is a historical reproduction of one of the earliest known Tarot decks.
  • Ancient Tarot of Marseilles is a reproduction that inspired the Waite-Smith deck.
  • Book of Thoth Etteilla Tarot is a reporduction of the Thoth decks, one of the first attempts at constructing an explicitly occult deck.
  • Cat's Eye Tarot Designed by a feline vetrinarian uses classic Waite-Smith suits and lightly interprets the symbols in a feline context
  • Mystical Cats Tarot Gorgeous artwork, heavily interpreted Waite-Smith. Rather than putting cats in classic scenes, it instead imagines the feelings of the scenes and puts them in a feline context. Suits are Earth, Sky, Fire, Sea
