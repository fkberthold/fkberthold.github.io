I collect Tarot decks because the art on them is interesting and I enjoy researching where it came from.

*** My Tarot Decks
- Aleister Crowley Thoth Tarot
  - Artist: Lady Frieda Harris
  - Design: Aleister Crowley
- [[http://www.aeclectic.net/tarot/cards/book-of-azathoth/][The Book of Azathoth Tarot]] by Nemo
- The Hermetic Tarot
  - Artist/Design: Godfrey Dowson
- Londa Tarot
  - Artist/Designer: Londa Marks
- Tarot in Wonderland
  - Artist: Eugene Smith
  - Design: Barbara Moore
- Waite-Smith (Rider-Waite)
  - Artist: Pamela Colman Smith
  - Design: A. E. Waite
- The Wonderland Tarot
  - Artist/Designer: Chris Abbey & Morgana Abbey

*** Tarot decks I admire but haven't gotten around to picking up
- [[http://www.aeclectic.net/tarot/cards/english-magic-tarot/][English Magic Tarot]] has this fantastic, flat imagery that looks simple on the surface but has a dozen stories.
- [[Thelema Tarot]] has beautiful images that each seem to glow.
- [[http://www.aeclectic.net/tarot/cards/lo-scarabeo/][Lo Scarabeo]] trys to blend the two major lineages of Tarot, The Waite-Smith and The Crowley Thoth.
- [[http://www.aeclectic.net/tarot/cards/visconti-sforza/][Visconti-Sforza Tarot]] is a historical reproduction of one of the earliest known Tarot decks.
- [[http://www.aeclectic.net/tarot/cards/ancient-marseilles/][Ancient Tarot of Marseilles]] is a reproduction that inspired the Waite-Smith deck.
- [[http://www.aeclectic.net/tarot/cards/book-of-thoth-etteilla/][Book of Thoth Etteilla Tarot]] is a reporduction of the Thoth decks, one of the first attempts at constructing an explicitly occult deck.
